About Me

I am a computer science student on my master's degree who is particularly passionate about software architecture and its design, as well as the smaller-scale interplay between classes, abstract classes and interfaces and how these can be built to provide maintainable, scalable and easy-to-read code.

I co-own a company named Bork with my brother where I build and maintain all of our software as the sole developer in the team. The main products that I work on are our stylish website, built with C# and ASP.NET, and our Discord bot, built with C# and Discord.Net. Through this work, I have gained invaluable experience as I have built large applications from scratch over the course of multiple years.

In my free time, I like to spend time on my Linux installation, learning new Vim motions and working on my Neovim setup to improve my efficiency in writing. Furthermore, I am currently learning to use the Dvorak keyboard layout which also spawned my interest in custom keyboards because the keyboard layout does not support the Danish characters æ, ø and å, and thus I had to customize my keyboard using QMK to accommodate both my desired keyboard layout and the Danish characters.
